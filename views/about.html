Our Story

Find your calm.

MA SPACE started with a simple idea: a home should feel like a place to exhale, not another thing to keep up with. We design and source small-batch furniture and objects in a quiet, considered palette — pieces meant to be lived with for years, not replaced every season.

Every item in our collection is chosen (or made) with the same questions in mind: does it hold up to daily life, does it age well, and does it earn its place in a room instead of just filling it. That's the whole design brief, really.

We're a small team working out of a converted foundry studio, and we still personally check every new piece before it goes on the shop floor — digital or otherwise.

Small batch

We produce in limited runs so quality never gets diluted by scale.

Considered materials

Solid wood, ceramic, and natural fiber — chosen to age gracefully.

Made to last

Every piece is built to be lived with for years, not one season.
